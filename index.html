Independent Facilitation Services Agreement
(Regional Center–Paid Services: 024 and RC-Paid 099 Only)

This Agreement is entered into by and between Empowered Futures Independent Facilitation LLC ("IF") and the SDP Participant or Authorized Representative ("Participant"), and is effective upon execution.

1. Purpose of Agreement

This Agreement defines the scope, terms, and conditions for Regional Center–authorized services, including Person-Centered Planning (Service Code 024) and Independent Facilitation paid by the RC under Service Code 099, pursuant to Welfare & Institutions Code §4685.8, Title 17 regulations, and applicable SDP guidance. Ongoing IF services funded through the Participant's SDP budget are governed by a separate SDP IF Agreement, if elected.

2. Clarification of Roles and Payment Source

Payment for services is made by the Regional Center and does not create a private-pay obligation for the Participant.

3. Scope of Services (RC-Paid Only)

024 – Person-Centered Planning: PCP creation, review of current supports, SDP feasibility and timelines, SDP education, service categories and compliance, and generic resource discussion.

099 – RC-Paid IF: Employer model consultation, staffing/scheduling and overtime planning, Spending Plan drafting and submission, FMS selection, provider enrollment, and coordination with the RC and FMS.

The IF does not provide direct care, supervision, employment, or legal services.

4. Billing Parameters

PCP (024): ~10 hours, ~$1,000.00 total as set by the RC. RC-paid IF (099): ~40 hours over 6 months at ~$50/hour. Additional hours are not typically approved; time extensions require RC approval.

5. Communication, Scheduling & Working Relationship

The IF uses HIPAA-compliant SMS, email, phone, and video conferencing. Appointments may be scheduled by calling/texting/emailing or via booking link. Typical business hours: Monday–Friday, 10:00 AM–5:00 PM. Services are primarily remote. All communication and billing activity is billed in 15-minute increments.

6. Timelines & RC Process Expectations
  • Typical timelines (not guarantees):
    • PCP Draft Meetings: 4–8 hours
    • PCP drafting: 5–7 business days after final PCP Draft Meeting
    • IPP scheduling: ~2 weeks
    • Formal Purchase Requests: 45 days from submission
    • SDP budget: ~30 days
    • Spending Plan approval: 2 weeks–1 month
    • FMS POS processing: ~2 weeks
    • SDP begins on 1st day of the month following
7. Participant Rights and Responsibilities

The Participant has the right to clear information about services, timelines, and billing. The Participant is responsible for providing accurate information, attending scheduled meetings, and communicating changes that may affect services.

8. Confidentiality

All Participant information is confidential and protected under HIPAA.

9. Billing Transparency

The IF maintains compliant documentation. Participants have the right to inspect billing; the Regional Center is the final billing authority.

10. Ending Services, Notice & Transition

The Participant may end services at any time. The IF will notify the RC, submit final billing, and provide completed PCPs and Spending Plans. Transition to a new IF will be coordinated as authorized.

11. Limitation of Liability

The IF is not responsible for decisions or actions of the RC, FMS, or third-party providers.

12. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by California law.

13. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement for RC-paid services.
